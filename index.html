Privacy Policy for Gogo Spoon

Effective date: July 09, 2026

Gogo Spoon ("the app") is developed by Minkyung Cho ("we", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses, how it's used, and your choices.

Information the app accesses

Location. When you use the "Check Nearby" or restaurant search features, the app asks for your device's location (foreground only — we never request or use "Always" background location access). Your location is used to:

  • Find restaurants near you
  • Filter and sort search results by distance

Your location is sent to Google's Places API as part of these requests (see "Third-party services" below). We do not store your location on any server we control, and we do not track your location history — each request uses your current position at the time you tap "Check Nearby" or search.

You can deny or revoke location access at any time in your device's Settings. The app will still work without it, except that nearby/distance-based features won't be available.

Restaurant data you create. The app lets you save restaurants (name, address, rating, your personal star rating, visit history, and notes on what you ordered). This data is stored only on your device, using local app storage. We do not upload it to any server, and we do not have access to it. If you delete the app, this data is permanently deleted along with it — there is currently no cloud backup or account system.

Search queries. When you type a restaurant name to search, that text is sent to Google's Places API to retrieve matching results. We do not store your search history ourselves.

Third-party services

The app uses the Google Places API to search for restaurants, show ratings/photos/addresses, and link to Google Maps. When you use search or nearby features, your query text and/or location coordinates are sent to Google. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Google Maps Platform's terms. We do not control how Google processes this data beyond what their policies describe.

What we don't do

  • We don't collect your name, email, or any account information — the app has no login or user accounts.
  • We don't use analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • We don't sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We don't have a server or database of our own — all your saved data lives only on your device.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes (for example, if we add cloud sync or accounts in the future), we'll update this page and revise the effective date above.
